When life gives you lemons, make lemonade. But you dont want lemonade, cause you are the chosen one - and no-one tells you what to do! Also, this evil dude kidnapped your girlfriend, and wants to blow up the world! Stop him by making all the things, and using them to defeat all the enemies!
Be aware tho, all the red barrels will explode! And watch out for arrows coming at your knees. Oh, and dont forget to avoid all the unnessary side and escort quests!
A game filled with cliche's... and lemons.
